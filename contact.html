Mung Bean Bakery was founded in 2019 by Cathy Li and Melanie Klock. They are both students at the University of Texas at Austin studying Neuroscience. Cathy has always had a passion and talent for baking all sorts of pastries. She utilizes her Asian roots to add little twists to her desserts. Melanie has always had a talent for eating such delicious baked goods, and together Cathy and Melanie make the perfect team!

You can contact the founders of Mung Bean Bakery at info@mungbeanbakery.com.
